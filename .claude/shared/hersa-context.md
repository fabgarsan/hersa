# Hersa — Company Context and Services Portfolio

Hersa is an education-sector company based in Cali, Colombia, specializing in end-to-end graduation ceremony services for schools and educational institutions.

**Headquarters:** Hersa Casa Campestre, Carrera 127° #20-22, Pance, Cali, Colombia.

**Business Model:**
- B2B: Educational institutions (client decision-makers are principals/rectors)
- B2C: Graduating students (end consumers of photography, entertainment, and apparel services)

---

## Complete Services Portfolio

**Graduation ceremony logistics:**
- Ceremony coordination and execution
- Ushers and ceremony staff
- Master of ceremony (external contractor)
- Graduation rehearsal and protocol management

**Photography studio and deliverables:**
- Studio location: Hersa Casa Campestre
- Per-graduate package (customizable by negotiation with institution):
  - 1 half-body enlargement 20×30 cm
  - 9 half-body photos 3×4 cm
  - 1 half-body with parchment 20×30 cm
  - 1 card-photo enlargement 20×30 cm
  - 1 group photo 20×30 cm
  - 1 group "loca" (fun) photo 20×30 cm
  - 1 poster 50×70 cm (unframed)
- Add-on services: extra posters (50,000 COP each), family photos (20,000 COP each), companion-group photos
- Photography is exclusive to graduates; family members do not attend photo shoots

**Graduation attire and accessories:**
- Graduation cap (birrete) + cord as keepsake
- Embroidered stole with institution logo
- Luxury button with institution shield
- Award medals (top students, if negotiated)

**Decoration and event setup:**
- Floral arrangements and auditorium decoration
- Tables, chairs, glassware, place settings
- Full event setup per venue and institution

**Invitation cards:**
- Single-use QR codes (one per guest)
- Count per graduate defined by institution in negotiation
- Scanned and invalidated at ceremony entrance

**Prom activity (Casa Campestre):**
- Full venue setup with Tiffany-style chairs, decoration, glassware
- Meal service: two-protein plate, rice, salad, soft beverages
- Professional servers and toast service
- Round-trip transport for graduates only (shared buses)
- Prom is exclusive to graduates; family members do not attend
- External catering provider (role: "Alimentos")

**Teachers dinner (Cena de Profesores):**
- Held at Hersa Casa Campestre
- Included in graduation package negotiation (no additional charge to institution/students)
- Service by temporary external staff (meseros)
- External catering provider delivers food and beverages

**Miscellaneous photography session (Toma Varios):**
- Cross-institution photo session for: add-on requests, dissatisfaction retakes (special cases), or absent students who missed both original shoot and Prom
- Held before graduation day
- Date published in advance to all institutions

**Diploma folder:**
- Branded with institution logo

**Serenade service** (external contractor, if negotiated)

**Transport:**
- Shared round-trip buses between institution groups on photo shoot day
- Buses depart at exact scheduled time without exceptions
- External conductor (driver) contractor

**Music entertainment** (external contractor, if negotiated)
